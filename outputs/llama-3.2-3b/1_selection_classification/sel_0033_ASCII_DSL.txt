### Solution
```sql
SELECT 
  x.name 
FROM 
  products x 
WHERE 
  x.category = 'stationery' 
  AND x.portable = True;
```

### Explanation
This SQL query selects the names of products that belong to the 'stationery' category and are portable. The `IN` operator is used to filter by category, and the `AND` operator is used to combine the two conditions. The `SELECT` statement is used to retrieve the `name` column of the filtered products.
